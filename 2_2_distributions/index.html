Ideological rankings, labor sympathies, party affiliation, and representation

The chart above visualizes multiple data points. Each dot represents a sitting U.S. Senator. Left to right shows ideological score from progressive to conservative. Top to bottom shows lifetime ranking from the AFL-CIO on support for organized labor. Color shows party affiliation (R = Republican, B = Democrat, G = Independent). Size is roughly proportional to the number of constiuents represented by the given senator.
